# 04 - How to Use napkin-lessons.md

## Happy story: mistakes became process improvements
Lia lost time once because she skipped a negative test. She captured it in napkin lessons and did not repeat it.

## What is yours (programmer)
- Write short practical lessons.
- Always add "action next time".

## What is the agent's
- Help convert events into clear lessons.
- Suggest prevention actions.

## Formula
- Lesson: what happened.
- Action: what you will do next time.
