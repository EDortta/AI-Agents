# 11 - Issue Management Fundamentals

## Happy story: less noise, better delivery
Lia learned that coding speed alone is not enough. Without clear issues, teams lose context, duplicate work, and break scope.

## What is yours (programmer)
- Turn requests into clear issues (goal, scope, risk, tests, DoD).
- Keep work linked with `work_id`.
- Keep status realistic.

## What is the agent's
- Help structure issue content with templates.
- Draft complete Jira/GitHub bodies.
- Keep local epic/task docs consistent.

## Minimum structure of a good issue
- Context
- Objective
- In scope
- Out of scope
- ARO (Acceptance, Risk, Operations)
- Test plan
- DoD

## Practical rule
A weak issue creates weak execution. Spend minutes clarifying and save hours later.
